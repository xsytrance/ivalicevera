## 1. STORY STRUCTURE

### The Four Chapters
FFT's story is divided into four chapters, progressing from political intrigue to supernatural climax:

**Chapter 1: "The Meager"**
- Introduces Ramza Beoulve, Delita Heiral, and the post-Fifty Years' War political landscape.
- The Corpse Brigade (peasant revolt) is active; Ramza encounters Wiegraf and Milleuda Folles.
- Key events: Battle of Ziekden Fortress, Tietra Heiral's death, Ramza deserts the Order.
- Ends with Ramza becoming a sellsword under the alias "Lugria."

**Chapter 2: "The Manipulative and the Subservient"**
- Princess Ovelia is kidnapped at Orbonne Monastery (framed as Goltanna's doing, actually Larg).
- Ramza pursues and allies with Agrias Oaks to protect Ovelia.
- Introduction of Mustadio Bunansa and the Zodiac Stones (auracite).
- Cardinal Delacroix reveals the Lucavi plot; transforms into Cúchulainn and is defeated.
- Delita manipulates events, framing Goltanna and triggering the War of the Lions.

**Chapter 3: "The Valiant"**
- Ramza is branded a heretic by the Church.
- Alma Beoulve joins him; they battle the Lucavi and Knights Templar.
- Orbonne Monastery holds the Scriptures of Germonique (evidence of Church corruption).
- Wiegraf, possessed by Belias, is defeated.
- Isilud Tengille captures Alma; taken to Riovanes Castle.
- Ramza defeats Belias; Folmarv Tengille takes Alma to Necrohol of Mullonde.
- Alliance with Delita and Count Cidolfus Orlandeau at Zeltennia Castle.

**Chapter 4: "In the Name of Love" / "Somebody to Love"**
- Climax of the War of the Lions and the Lucavi threat.
- Fort Besselat: Barich Fendsor uses Mossfungus poison; Ramza stops him.
- Delita assassinates Duke Goltanna and (a fake) Thunder God.
- Dycedarg murders Duke Larg; kills Zalbaag after being exposed.
- Ramza fights Dycedarg → Adrammelech transformation; House Beoulve falls.
- Limberry: Elmdore reveals Alma was never there; transforms into Zalera.
- Meliadoul Tengille defects to Ramza after witnessing the Lucavi.
- Mullonde Necrohol: Folmarv betrays Ramza, revives undead Zalbaag.
- Airship Graveyard: Folmarv → Hashmal transformation; sacrificed to revive Saint Ajora.
- Alma possessed by Ajora → Ultima, the High Seraph.
- Ramza defeats Ultima; the party escapes via auracite teleportation.
- Epilogue: Delita becomes King; Ovelia attacks him (ends ambiguously).
- Orran Durai writes the Durai Papers; burned as a heretic.
- Centuries later, Arazlam Durai rediscovers them.

## 2. KEY CHARACTERS

### Protagonists

**Ramza Beoulve** (aka Ramza Lugria)
- Youngest son of House Beoulve, a prestigious 300-year military dynasty.
- Mother was common-born; half-brothers Zalbaag and Dycedarg are from a noble mother.
- Trained at Royal Military Academy in Gariland with Delita Heiral and Tietra Heiral.
- Believes in justice over loyalty to corrupt institutions.
- Rejects his family name after Dycedarg poisons their father and Argath kills Tietra.
- Becomes a sellsword under Goffard Gaffgarion (alias "Lugria").
- Ultimate fate: Survives via auracite teleportation with Alma; leaves Ivalice.

**Delita Heiral**
- Ramza's childhood friend; commoner adopted into House Beoulve.
- Sister Tietra Heiral dies at Ziekden Fortress (killed by Argath).
- Ambitious, pragmatic, willing to manipulate for the greater good.
- Kidnaps Ovelia as part of a political scheme — motives are complex.
- Becomes commander of the Southern Sky, assassinates Goltanna.
- Crowned King of Ivalice after the war.
- Final scene: Ovelia attacks him; mutual stabbing — ambiguous ending.

**Agrias Oaks**
- Holy Knight of the Lionsguard, sworn to protect Princess Ovelia.
- Cousin of Meliadoul Tengille.
- Initially distrusts Ramza (he's a sellsword/heretic) but joins after learning the truth.
- Loyal, honorable, duty-bound.
- One of Ramza's most steadfast allies.

**Mustadio Bunansa**
- Machinist from Clockwork City of Dorter (Goug).
- Pursued by Baert Trading Company over Zodiac Stones.
- Joins Ramza after the party helps his father Besrudio.
- Can disable traps and open locked pathways.

**Rapha & Marach Galthena**
- Sibling seers from the assassin group Khamja.
- Adopted by Grand Duke Gerrith Barrington, who razed their village.
- Rapha (younger sister, mage) and Marach (older brother, healer).
- Join Ramza after learning the truth about their adoptive father.

**Count Cidolfus Orlandeau** ("The Thunder God")
- Legendary general of the Order of the Southern Sky.
- One of the most powerful warriors in Ivalice.
- Framed by High Confessor Marcel; branded a heretic.
- Joins Ramza at Zeltennia Castle.
- Key role in the final battles.

**Meliadoul Tengille**
- Divine Knight; Agrias's cousin.
- Daughter of Folmarv Tengille (Knights Templar leader / Lucavi host).
- Initially pursues Ramza to avenge her brother Isilud.
- Defects to Ramza after witnessing her father's Lucavi transformation.
- Last story-related recruit.

**Alma Beoulve**
- Ramza's younger sister; different mothers but close.
- Loyal despite family's opposition to Ramza.
- Captured by the Templars; possessed by Ultima/Ajora.
- Freed after Ramza defeats Ultima; survives.

### Supporting Characters

**Princess Ovelia Atkascha**
- Adopted daughter of King Ondoria III; heir to the throne.
- Manipulated as a political pawn by both Orders.
- Distrusts those who use her for power.
- Marries Delita after the war; final confrontation is tragic.

**Argath Thadalfus**
- Noble knight; servant of Marquis Messam Elmdore.
- Rescued by Ramza from the Corpse Brigade.
- Contemptuous of commoners; kills Tietra Heiral at Ziekden Fortress.
- Killed by Ramza and Delita in retaliation.
- Later revived as undead, attacks at Limberry.

**Wiegraf Folles**
- Leader of the Corpse Brigade (peasant revolt).
- Sister Milleuda Folles killed fighting Ramza.
- Vows vengeance; later possessed by Belias (Lucavi).
- Defeated at Orbonne Monastery.

### Antagonists

**Dycedarg Beoulve**
- Ramza's eldest half-brother; leads House Beoulve.
- Poisoned their father Barbaneth.
- Orders Argath to kill Tietra.
- Possessed by Adrammelech (Lucavi) at Eagrose Castle.
- Kills Zalbaag; defeated by Ramza.

**Zalbaag Beoulve**
- Another half-brother; involved in court politics.
- Discovers Dycedarg poisoned their father.
- Killed by Dycedarg → Adrammelech.
- Revived as undead by Folmarv; begs Ramza to kill him again.

**Folmarv Tengille**
- Leader of the Knights Templar; secretly a Lucavi host.
- Murders his own son Isilud.
- Captures Alma; orchestrates the Lucavi resurrection plan.
- Transforms into Hashmal; sacrifices himself to revive Ultima.

**Cardinal Alphonse Delacroix**
- Senior Church of Glabados official.
- Allied with Duke Larg; seeks Zodiac Stones for power.
- Transforms into Cúchulainn (Lucavi) using auracite; defeated by Ramza.

**High Confessor Marcel Funebris**
- Head of the Church of Glabados.
- Manipulative; seeks theocratic control of Ivalice.
- Revolts against the Templars; fatally wounded.

**Goffard Gaffgarion**
- Ramza's former mercenary mentor.
- Hired by Dycedarg to kill Princess Ovelia.
- Betrays Ramza; defeated in single combat at Golgollada.

### The Lucavi (Demons)
- Immortal dark gods banished by the Zodiac Braves.
- Can possess hosts using Zodiac Stones (auracite).
- Key Lucavi encountered:
  - **Belias** (fire) — possessed Wiegraf
  - **Cúchulainn** (bloodshed) — possessed Delacroix
  - **Adrammelech** (lightning) — possessed Dycedarg
  - **Zalera** (death) — possessed Elmdore
  - **Hashmal** (order) — possessed Folmarv
  - **Ultima** (final boss) — possessed Alma/Ajora

## 3. WORLD & LORE

### Setting: Ivalice
- Medieval kingdom of seven territories recovering from the Fifty Years' War.
- Society is class-divided: noble families rule over a weak monarchy.
- Real power: Church of Glabados (worships Saint Ajora, commands Knights Templar).

### The Fifty Years' War
- Conflict between Ivalice and Ordallia over Ordallian succession.
- Lasted half a century; ended in near-defeat for Ivalice.
- Resulted in crippling debts, reparations, territorial losses.
- Mass unemployment among returning soldiers; distrust of aristocracy.
- Ramza's father Barbaneth was a hero of this war.

### The War of the Lions (Civil War)
- Triggered by King Ondoria III's death.
- Two factions:
  - **Order of the Northern Sky** (Duke Larg, White Lion) — supports Prince Orinus
  - **Order of the Southern Sky** (Duke Goltanna, Black Lion) — supports Princess Ovelia
- Named for the lion crests of both houses.
- Orchestrated by the Church to weaken both sides.
- Ends with Delita crowned King.

### Zodiac Stones (Auracite)
- Mythical relics from an ancient age; each bears a zodiac sign.
- Used by legendary Zodiac Braves to banish the Lucavi.
- Can manifest a user's deepest wish (miracle or demonic power).
- Possession by Lucavi occurs when a user channels their power.
- Collected throughout the game by the Church and various factions.

### The Church of Glabados
- State religion led by High Confessor Marcel.
- Commands the Knights Templar (led by Folmarv).
- Orchestrated the War of the Lions to seize power.
- Suppressed the truth about Ramza (branded him a heretic).
- Scriptures of Germonique hidden beneath Orbonne Monastery reveal corruption.

### The Durai Papers
- Written by Orran Durai over five years.
- Expose Ramza as the true hero (contradicting Church narrative).
- Orran burned at the stake as a heretic.
- Rediscovered centuries later by Arazlam Durai.
- In-game Chronicles narrated by Arazlam present both accounts.

## 4. GAME MECHANICS (For Context)

### Job System
- Characters gain XP (levels) and JP (job points).
- Advanced jobs require mastering prerequisites.
- Skills learned in one job can be used in others.
- Story characters have exclusive special jobs (Agrias = Holy Knight).

### Key Jobs
**Physical:** Squire, Knight, Archer, Monk, Thief, Dragoon, Samurai, Ninja, Dancer
**Magical:** Chemist, White Mage, Black Mage, Mystic, Time Mage, Orator, Summoner, Bard, Arithmetician
**Special:** Holy Knight, Fell Knight, Dark Knight, Onion Knight, Assassin, Templar

### Zodiac Signs
- 12 zodiac signs influence combat matchups.
- Opposing pairs deal increased damage (e.g., male Pisces vs. female Virgo).
- Relevant for both story (Zodiac Stones) and gameplay.

### ATB System
- Units act when Charge Time bar fills (based on Speed).
- Spells/abilities have charge times.
- Direction matters: rear attacks more effective.

## 5. STORY PROGRESS MARKER (Save File)

In the 40KB `resume_*_main.sav` files:
- **Offset 0x38**: Chapter indicator (u8)
- **Offset 0x39**: Scene indicator (u8)
- **Offset 0x3b**: Battle state flag (0 = pre-battle, 1 = post-battle)
- **Offset 0x3c**: Checkpoint/counter value

Known values:
- `0x10, 0x09` = Early game (Chapter 1-2 area, early scenes)
- `0x15, 0x18` = Mid game (Chapter 2-3 area, later scenes)

The 2MB `fftsave.bin` contains all party member data, character markers, and extended region with per-character metadata at 0x31000-0x45000.

### Character Presence Identification
- Look for name strings in the 0x31000+ region of fftsave.bin
- Known party members and their offsets (from Archer save):
  - Xsy:    0x318a8
  - Ghost:  0x31b00
  - Arza:   0x31d58
  - OWL:    0x32910
  - Snow:   0x31fb0
  - (Ramza, Agrias, Delita, others may be at different offsets)

Each character record (0x31000+ region) contains:
- 40× u16 stat block (pre-name, 80 bytes)
- Name string + extra field
- Metadata: [slot_id] [00] [unit_type] [FF] + padding
